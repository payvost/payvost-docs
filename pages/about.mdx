# About Payvost

Payvost is a leading payment infrastructure platform that empowers businesses to accept payments seamlessly across the globe.

## Our Mission

We're building the future of payments - making it simple, secure, and accessible for businesses of all sizes to accept payments and manage their financial operations.

## Why Payvost?

- **Developer-First**: Built by developers, for developers
- **Global Reach**: Support for 100+ countries and multiple currencies
- **Security**: Bank-grade security and PCI-DSS compliance
- **Innovation**: Cutting-edge technology and continuous improvements
